Who We Are

Building More Than Athletes

Tri-State Elite Athletics, Inc. is a nonprofit AAU girls basketball program based in Berkeley County, West Virginia, built to fill a critical gap in competitive athletic opportunities for young women in the region.

Girls basketball in Berkeley County is severely underfunded and underserved. We exist to change that — providing structured training, mentorship, and access to regional competition for girls ages 8 to 11 who have the talent and drive to grow beyond entry-level play.

Our program builds confidence, discipline, teamwork, and competitive excellence — while actively creating pathways to scholarships, NIL opportunities, and higher-level collegiate exposure for every girl on this roster.

Our Mission

Developing confidence, discipline, teamwork, and competitive excellence — putting Berkeley County back on the map for girls' basketball.

We compete in regional AAU events throughout West Virginia, Maryland, Virginia, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey — giving our athletes the visibility and opportunity that simply didn't exist here before. Every girl who steps on this court is being built for something bigger.

Beyond the Court

Real Pathways. Real Futures.

Tri-State Elite was built with the bigger picture in mind. We're not just developing basketball players — we're developing young women whose talent, work ethic, and visibility will open doors to opportunities that extend far beyond the gym.

We believe every girl in Berkeley County deserves the same shot as athletes in bigger markets. We're here to make sure they get it — by building their skills, their exposure, and their futures right here at home. From NIL deals to Division I scholarship pipelines, TSE is planting seeds today that will grow for years to come.

Meet the Staff

The Coaches Behind the Vipers

Associate Head Coach
Coach J Newman

Coach J has had a basketball in his hands since the age of 3. Growing up in Loudoun County, VA in a basketball family, he competed against and alongside players who went on to the NBA — and has coached in organizations that produced NBA and D1 stars who are big names to this day. His pedigree is real and his passion is contagious. Coach J holds the girls to a high standard and brings a fire and intensity to every practice that pushes every athlete to reach their ceiling. As the primary on-court presence, his impact is felt in every single rep.

🎣 Fun Fact Coach J is a fishing legend. One summer, he and his daughter caught 120 catfish together — including a 10 lb monster. The man never misses, on the court or on the water.
Head Coach & Program Director
Coach A. Steele

Coach A's story is proof that determination rewrites any timeline. She picked up basketball in 8th grade, stepped away, then came back in 10th with a vengeance — training all summer and earning the starting spot as captain of her varsity team, playing every quarter. She went on to red shirt for the FDU Knights. Now she pours that same relentless energy into every girl she coaches. Coach A creates a safe space where athletes are pushed to their limits and loved through the process — tough, motherly, and completely invested in every player she imprints on.

🎨 Fun Fact Off the court, Coach A is a full creative — painting, pottery, and making art. She lives what she preaches: athletes can be anything they set their mind to.
Assistant Coach
Coach Baze

Coach Baze's coaching journey started the way the best ones do — by stepping up when his team needed him most. What began as filling in became a calling. Originally from Baltimore, MD, he relocated to Berkeley County in 2016 and has been a pillar in the local basketball community ever since. He's coached his daughter through her entire career, winning championships in 5 of his 6 seasons at the helm. Calm, patient, and deeply instructional, Coach Baze has a rare gift for development — pushing every girl to be measurably better each time she steps on the floor.

🏆 Fun Fact Coach Baze won championships in 5 out of 6 coaching seasons — and his proudest project is his daughter, who he's developing to be the best player in all of West Virginia. 😄
